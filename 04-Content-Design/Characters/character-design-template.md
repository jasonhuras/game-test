## Example Character: "Vorrath the Blood Knight"

### High-Level Concept
**One-Sentence Pitch**: "A lifesteal tank who gets stronger as he damages enemies, rewarding aggressive play."

**Archetype**: Tank/Bruiser

**Playstyle**: Aggressive sustain, dive into enemies

**Fantasy**: Unkillable warrior who heals by dealing damage

---

### Visual Design
**Appearance**: Heavy crimson armor, glowing red eyes, dual-wielding swords

**Color Palette**: Red (primary), Black (secondary), Gold (accent)

**Silhouette**: Broad shoulders, heavy armor, imposing stance

---

### Stats (Base Values)

| Stat | Value | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Max Health | 150 | 50% more than baseline |
| Movement Speed | 4.5 m/s | Slightly slower (heavy armor) |
| Damage | 8 | Lower base damage |
| Attack Speed | 0.9x | Slower attacks |
| Critical Chance | 3% | Lower crit |
| Critical Damage | 2.0x | Standard |

---

### Starting Equipment
**Weapon**: Dual Blood Swords (Melee)
**Why**: Fits vampire/blood knight theme, encourages close combat

---

### Unique Passive Ability
**Passive Name**: Blood Pact

**Description**: Heal for 5% of all damage dealt

**Mechanical Effect**: Built-in lifesteal, stacks with augments

**Design Intent**: Rewards aggressive play, creates sustain tank playstyle

---

### Character-Specific Augments

**Augment 1: Crimson Fury** (Epic)
- Effect: +50% lifesteal, -20% max health
- Why: High-risk, high-reward build option

**Augment 2: Blood Boil** (Rare)
- Effect: Gain +2% damage per enemy hit in last 3 seconds (max 50%)
- Why: Rewards fighting in crowds

**Augment 3: Vampiric Dominance** (Legendary)
- Effect: Overheal becomes temporary shield (max 50% of max health)
- Why: Unique sustain mechanic

---

### Unlock Conditions
**How to Unlock**: Reach Wave 10 with any character, then purchase for 500 Soul Essence

**Unlock Difficulty**: Easy (starter character)

---

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths**:
1. Excellent sustain through lifesteal
2. Tanky, can take hits
3. Great against hordes (more enemies = more healing)

**Weaknesses**:
1. Lower damage output than pure DPS
2. Slower movement, harder to kite
3. Struggles if can't hit enemies (ranged bosses)

---

### Recommended Builds

**Build 1: Immortal Tank**
- Core Augments: Max Health +50%, Damage Reduction +20%, Blood Boil
- Playstyle: Facetank everything, heal faster than you take damage
- Power Spike: Mid-game (Wave 10+)

**Build 2: Blood Mage**
- Core Augments: Spell Power +30%, Spell Vampire, Crimson Fury
- Playstyle: Mix melee with spells, heal off both
- Power Spike: Late-game (Wave 15+)

---

This character is designed for players who want to feel unkillable and enjoy aggressive, in-your-face combat.
